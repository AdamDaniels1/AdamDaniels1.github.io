I like to read and have read over 1260 books (so far). Much of my reading has been accomplished via speed-reading and in order to give you an idea of how long a book would take you to read given your reading speed I have created this simple calculator.

Enter the number of words in the book and your reading speed to calculate how long it will take you to read the book:

Don't know your reading speed? Go here and take the free test if you want to know.

I read about 660 words per minute but in order to have the best comprehension I slow myself down considerably in order to understand more difficult texts.

At 300 pages average per book, across 1200 books, that's roughly 360000 pages. If I plug my information into the calculator it took me roughly 2500 hours to read all of those books. That's 104 24 hour days! I have since wound down the amount of reading I do so I can concentrate on building things and working with technology.
